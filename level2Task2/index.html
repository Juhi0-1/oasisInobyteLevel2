APJ Abdul Kalam



1931-2015


Things which I admire the most



His vision and commitment to the development of India: Kalam was a scientist and a statesman who was deeply committed to the development of his country. He was passionate about using technology and innovation to solve the country's problems and make it a developed nation.


His humility and simplicity: Kalam was known for his humble and down-to-earth personality, despite achieving great success and holding high positions in government. He was always approachable and had a way of connecting with people from all walks of life.


His inspirational leadership: Kalam's leadership style was based on integrity, vision, and inspiration. He was a great motivator and mentor to many young people, encouraging them to pursue their dreams and work hard to achieve their goals.


His contributions to the field of science and technology: Kalam was a renowned scientist who made significant contributions to the development of India's missile program and space technology. He was also a strong advocate for the use of technology for the betterment of society.


His dedication to education: Kalam was a strong advocate for education and believed that it was the key to unlocking India's potential. He worked tirelessly to promote education and literacy, especially among young people and in rural areas.
